Knotle

A calm daily loop puzzle.

Every day, everyone gets the same board. Rotate the glowing tiles until every path joins into a single closed loop — and when it clicks shut, the whole loop lights up. Easy to learn in seconds, quietly satisfying to master.

  • One puzzle a day — a small ritual, not a time sink.
  • Chase the best possible solve to earn a Perfect.
  • Keep a streak, and share your result spoiler-free.
  • Unlimited Practice whenever you want more.
  • No ads. No tracking. Works offline — your data never leaves your device.
