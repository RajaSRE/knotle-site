Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Knotle is designed to respect your privacy completely. It collects nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. Knotle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account or sign-up is required or offered.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
  • No data is sent to us or to any third party.

Data stored on your device

Your game data — streaks, statistics, selected theme, and settings — is stored only on your device. It is shared privately with the Knotle home-screen widget through a private App Group container so the widget can show your streak. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Purchases

Knotle+ is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Your purchase entitlement is managed by the App Store.

Children

Knotle is suitable for all ages and collects no data, so it is safe for children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes — for example if a future version introduces optional advertising — we will update this page and the app's App Store privacy details accordingly.
